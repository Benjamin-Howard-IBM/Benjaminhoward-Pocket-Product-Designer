# research

UX research and strategy skill for Pocket Product Designer.

Use it to set UX strategy (vision, goals, plans, roadmaps), choose a research methodology with explicit pros/cons/tradeoffs, and write decision-grade research plans. Grounded in Nielsen Norman Group's UX Strategy study guide and its linked articles.

## Example prompts

```text
What research method should I use to find out why activation is low?
```

```text
Help me write a UX vision statement for our onboarding redesign.
```

```text
Draft a research plan to validate our new navigation before we build it.
```

```text
Which prioritization method should I use for our roadmap themes, and why?
```
