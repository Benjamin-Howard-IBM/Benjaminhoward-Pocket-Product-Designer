# Research

Load research and strategy instructions from:

- `skill/research/SKILL.md`
- `skill/research/resources/ux-strategy-study-guide.md`
- `skill/research/resources/research-methodologies.md`
- `skill/research/resources/sample-research-plan.md`

Infer the decision and main uncertainty first, then recommend the lowest-effort method or artifact that answers it. Recommend one primary method unless a complement covers a specific gap. Always state tradeoffs and a fallback. Keep strategy work at planning fidelity unless the user asks for higher fidelity. Ground claims in research or labeled assumptions and attach confidence where it matters.
