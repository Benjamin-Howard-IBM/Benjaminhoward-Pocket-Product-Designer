PAM Tooling Strategy - RFI Questionnaire
Privilege Access Management - Request For Information Summary
Lockheed Martin is currently exploring next-generation 
solutions to support a robust defense in depth security 
Privileged Access Management (PAM) 
portfolio.  As such, Lockheed Martin is soliciting a selection of vendors, seeking information relative to this effort.  Information obtained from this 
questionnaire will be used to determine eligible candidates which will be evaluated by Lockheed Martin Corporate Information Security (CIS) and internal 
partners from different business areas.   Post evaluation, the solutions presented may be called upon for a demonstration/workshop with the Lockheed 
Martin team.  Lockheed Martin invites you to participate in the following Request for Information (RFI) questionnaire, relative to your portfolio of privilege 
access management technologies, tools, and capabilities.
